"The Engraving on Glass – the Frozen Eternity in All Its Depth and Fragility ..."

Vasyl Pilka

To conquer the world of glass!

To make glass to be obedient to the hands of Master. To cut the engravings in glass as on copper, as in a tree. It was an ambitious goal. Vasyl began to realize it with a great enthusiasm. The glass subjected not at once. Long years of researches and experiments.

The capricious material crushed by dust, embossed eyes, exploded into face by the splinters of practically completed works.
But Ukrainian steel-maker, the experienced mechanic, the descendant of the Transcarpathia masters didn’t give up. And here the completed works became to appear from under the hand of the Master. The magic glass played by its fanciful reflections.
The unique technology of Vasyl Pilka permits to work with glass as with the plastic material. The understanding of physics and chemistry of materials allowed create the author’s (original) complex of the approaches, methods and techniques of the artistic carving on the glass massive by the diamond. At the present time Vasyl Pilka made more than three hundred works. Among them are both the original works and the engravings’ copies of the great masters of the past. The new horizons, goals, tasks, new projects and discoveries are ahead.
